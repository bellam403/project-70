About

Our Motto

"Hard Work,Discipline and Wisdom"is our motto.All students are trained and brought up in such a way that it makes the journey of education a successful one for them.Our ultimate aim is to build a community for personal growth,academic excellence and global citizenship

Location

Our Institutions are located an expanse of 16 acres with a serene and sensuous backdrop,at postal nagar,Bodhupatty,just 1km from Lord Anjaneya Temple in Namakkal Town

Our Vision

Our Vision is to Inspire,Innovate and Inculcate outstanding Education in every learner.

To help the childern become self confident,committed and self-reliant.

To make the students participate in all aspects of learning process which makes learning meaningful and enjoyable.

Hostel with Hospitality

Hostel facility is available only for girls from std XI to XII (CBSE and State Board) We follow a prescribed schedule which is strictly adhered in order to invigorate discipline in their routine in creating a healthy life style.

Salient features of our hostel

▪ Spacious and well ventilated rooms make our Hostel a home in itself.

▪ A/C Hostel rooms are available keeping in view of the necessity of conducive and comfortable study and learning atmosphere to the student

▪ Under the encompassing supervision of Boarding Teachers, every resident student at GP experiences emotional security, guidance and comfort with parental care.

▪ A warden manages students' affairs and helps them in every aspect of hostel life.

▪ Boarders are taught the art of dressing to perfection, presenting themselves in a neat, tidy and orderly manner. Passionately ingrained habit helps them acquire our culture and tradition in a delicate way.

▪ A skilled physician visits the school regularly and the nurses available in the hostel premises take care of the boarding children at all times.

▪ Supervised study hours are carried out in the hostels both in the mornings and evenings.

• Weekly phone turns enable boarders to stay in touch with their parents.

▪ Canteen facility offers paid service to the residents. Lip-smacking and mouth watering snacks, fresh juices and health drinks are made available.

AUDITORIUM

The school has a modern, aesthetically designed, centrally air-conditioned and majestic Auditorium. It is equipped with the latest sound and lighting systems and can accommodate more than 1000 persons at a sitting. It provides an elegant and State-of-the-art venue for Seminars, Debates, Discussions, Interschool competitions, Parent - Teacher meetings, Cultural Fests, Annual Day Celebrations and the other events.

LIBRARY

The school features three full-fledged libraries which house a wide variety of books and journals that provide students with opportunities to discover the world. They prove to be tranquil environs where children can come and satiate their quest for knowledge. To inculcate reading habit among students, a regular library period is allotted for classes upto IX std. Additions of the latest arrivals are constantly made in order to keep the library abreast of the trend.

LABORATORIES

To enhance the learning ability and skill and impart practical exposure in the field of Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Computer Science, the school has a number of well equipped laboratories furnished with latest instruments. Students in a hands-on-science program will understand the subject better and feel a sense of accomplishment once understanding is applied practically.

Achievements of Green Park Educational Institutions, NAMAKKAL

WHAT'S NEW

With the Divine Blessings of Lord Anjaneya, Green Park Educational Institutions were established in 2007 at Postal Nagar, Bodhupatty. With a unique record of creating innumerable Medicos and Engineers for more than a decade, Green Park is a stand-out institution in the state. Dedicated itself to propelling the students to excellence in Education along with "All Round Development's, Team Green Park relentlessly work on integrated education with innovative use of science and technology to develop world class citizens, through a holistic approach. With a team of well - qualified, extensively trained, highly passionate and strongly committed faculty, the Institution constantly encourages its students to explore beyond the curriculum. Being a shrine for moulding the mind and behaviour of the students, Green Park, as a milestone of great achievement, has enabled 533 students joined MBBS through NEET and 289 students joined the reputed Engineering Colleges in the state in the 2018 Batch.

The Management regularly monitors the academic progress of all the students. Special remedial programmes are conducted for late-bloomers. Our Trust is so magnanimous that it offers free education to 200 meritorious students every year for those who excel in X-Std Board Exams. Apart from this, our Trust liberally contributes for various charitable activities for the welfare of our society and thus humanity. With unflinching fervour and unabated passion, we run Green Park Senior Secondary School which, as usual with our Institutions, caters to the needs of students dreaming to learn through CBSE stream. Carving a niche for ourselves in the domain of Education is always our passion.
